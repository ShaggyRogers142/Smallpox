Purpose of This Arcticle (Thesis)


  • To give background information about the development of the smallpox vaccine

    • Thesis:

    In the mid 20th century the United States developed a diplomatic strategy to tackling the international issue of the infectious disease smallpox; through the diplomatic efforts of the United States the U.S. and other nations waged a successful campaign to eradicate the scourge of smallpox in the United States and around the world, laying the stage for future such efforts to tackle future endemic and pandemic diseases.
